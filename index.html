chainsawMan

"Chainsaw Man" is a popular Japanese manga written and illustrated by Tatsuki Fujimoto.
It was initially serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shonen Jump magazine from December 2018 to December 2020,
and then on Shueisha's Shonen Jump+ digital platform starting in July 2022.
The anime based on the manga premiered in October 2022, produced by studio MAPPA.
